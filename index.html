What is Ladki Bahin Yojana?

Mukhyamantri Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana is a flagship financial empowerment scheme launched by the Government of Maharashtra in July 2024. It provides ‚Çπ1,500 per month directly into the Aadhaar-linked bank accounts of eligible women, aiming to improve financial independence, health, nutrition, and decision-making power for women across Maharashtra.

üí° Good News for 2026 Beneficiaries
Pending installments (including combined December‚ÄìJanuary payments) of ‚Çπ3,000 or ‚Çπ4,500 are being credited directly into Aadhaar-linked accounts. Ensure your eKYC is complete to receive all pending amounts.

The scheme was designed specifically for the socio-economic empowerment of women, ensuring that money reaches beneficiaries directly without any middlemen through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) system. Since its launch, the scheme has enrolled millions of women across Maharashtra.

eKYC Process ‚Äì Step by Step Guide

üö® eKYC is MANDATORY in 2026
Without completing eKYC, your ‚Çπ1,500 monthly payment will be permanently stopped. If your account shows "Hold" or "Temporary Pause," it is due to incomplete eKYC. Re-activation is possible ‚Äî you do NOT need to re-apply from scratch.

The Maharashtra Government has made the eKYC (Electronic Know Your Customer) process fully online and accessible from home. Follow these steps carefully:

  1. 1
    Visit the Official eKYC Page
    Go to ladakibahin.maharashtra.gov.in/ekyc
  2. 2
    Enter Your Aadhaar Number
    Type your 12-digit Aadhaar number and enter the CAPTCHA code shown on screen.
  3. 3
    Agree to Aadhaar Authentication
    Tick the "I agree" checkbox for Aadhaar-based authentication and click Send OTP.
  4. 4
    Enter OTP
    You will receive an OTP on your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. Enter it promptly. If not received, click "Resend OTP."
  5. 5
    Father's / Husband's Aadhaar
    Enter the Aadhaar number of your father or husband as required. A second OTP will be sent ‚Äî enter it and submit.
  6. 6
    Select Caste Category
    Choose your applicable caste category. Read Declaration A.S. No. 1 & 2 carefully and select the correct option.
  7. 7
    Tick & Submit
    Tick the confirmation box and click Submit. A success notification will confirm your eKYC is complete. Take a screenshot.
